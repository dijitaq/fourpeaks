If it’s lifestyle you want look no further.

Call me bias, but the beauty of being a local in the area is that you get to know everything that’s great about it and our region is no exception. With more coffee shops than you can poke a stick at, amazing pubs that invite you in with a fine glass of red, plus the largest natural theme park with loads of activities to embark on. Our community is small in size with big hearts and it’s reassuring that everyone knows everyone. There is a real community spirit.
